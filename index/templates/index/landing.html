The Problem

If you're here chances are you're probably feeling stuck and like you've wasted all available time this year.

Chances are you probably had a journal with goals you set for this year ("new year resolutions"), but each time you look at them you feel disappointed in yourself, for not acheiving the progress you said you would acheive.

Chances are you've been taking classes and learning a lot, but you're struggling to find the motivation to put it in action. You want to grow, you want to become a better version of yourself, no, you want to become the best version of yourself.

We all do. The Problem? We don't know how. We don't know the right way to follow or maybe we do, but we are finding it hard to implement it. Feeling like giving up? Like these big dreams are not just what you're here for?

The Solution

Don't worry, help has arrived. You can still make the best out the rest of the year. The good news is your growth can be elevated, if you're willing to accept it.

The Growth Series,2024, is coming to elevate you in a way, you least expect. A three part journey, starting in October, peaking in November and finally coming to an end (on a high note, of course) in December.

It aims to take a three step method towards helping you grow, which include mindset shifting, awakening and discipline building/fortification.

As part of the many activities, there'll also be a book club, to allow you improve your reading and implementation habits (readers are leaders, after all).
